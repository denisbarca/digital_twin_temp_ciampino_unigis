Digital Twin - Temperatures in Ciampino

Information

A digital twin is presented here, specifically for the city of Ciampino (a town in the province of Rome), focusing on the management and planning of potential actions to mitigate recorded temperatures based on historical data. The analysis was conducted using satellite images dated 2023/08/23, which were then processed to calculate the Land Surface Temperature (LST) for estimating thermal conditions.

The displayed map includes various layers related to the territory, which are listed below:

  • The layer for municipal administrative boundaries;
  • The layer for buildings (characterized by a dual 2D/3D nature depending on the zoom level and by LOD 1.1);
  • The layer for trees and more continuous arboreal vegetation (characterized by a dual 2D/3D nature depending on the zoom level and by LOD 1.1 and taken from Land Monitoring Service of Copernicus project);
  • The vector layer of the calculated LST value (using Landsat 8 imagery);
  • The layer related to land use for the territory (from Land Monitoring Service of Copernicus project).
